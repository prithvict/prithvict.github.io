Our Products

Black Mass
Our Black Mass contains Lithium, Cobalt, Nickel, and Manganese along with Graphite. This Black Mass contains less than 1% impurities.
Lithium (Li)
Our high-quality micronized Lithium Carbonate is extracted from used Lithium-ion batteries. This Lithium Carbonate is ideal for applications ranging from battery cells to glass and ceramics manufacturing.
Cobalt (Co)
Our premium-grade Cobalt Sulphate is extracted from end-of-life Lithium-ion batteries and is ideally suited to the production of Cobalt-containing cathode materials for Lithium-ion as well as Lithium polymer battery cells.
Nickel (Ni)
Our high-purity Nickel Sulphate is extracted from spent Lithium-ion batteries and is perfect for the production of Lithium-ion and Lithium polymer batteries.
Manganese (Mn)
Our high-grade Manganese Carbonate is extracted from spent Lithium-ion batteries and is a viable choice for use in many chemical applications as well as in the manufacture of Lithium-ion battery cells.
